The Fromagerie

Appenzell Innerrhoden
Five generations

Founded in 1887, Alpage has never left the valley, never scaled for supermarkets, and never changed its spring water.

Our herd of sixty Brown Swiss cows grazes the high meadows of the Säntis massif from May to October. At altitude, the grass is richer and the milk carries a fat and protein profile no lowland dairy can match.

Every morning that milk meets the copper vat within the hour. The curd is cut by hand, pressed overnight, and carried down into sandstone caves cut in 1903 — where each wheel rests at a constant 12 °C for twelve to eighteen months, turned and brushed by hand until its copper rind is ready.
